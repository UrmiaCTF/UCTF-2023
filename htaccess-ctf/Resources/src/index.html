one/.htaccess

RewriteEngine On
RewriteCond %{HTTP_HOST} !^localhost$
RewriteRule ".*" "-" [F]

two/.htaccess

RewriteEngine On

Your job is to bypass these restrictions and view the flag files anyway. Good luck!
